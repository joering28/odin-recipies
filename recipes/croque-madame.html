Croque Madame

What Is Croque Madame?

Croque Madame is a french inspired brunch sandwich that is more than just a ham and cheese. It is made up of a creamy cheese sauce and a hearty ham sandwich.

Ingredients for Sauce

  • Butter
  • 1 1/2 Tbs. Flour
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 2 Oz. Shredded Gruyère Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg

Ingredients for Sandwich

  • Sliced Bread
  • Sliced Ham
  • Eggs
  • Dijon Mustard

Directions for Sauce

  1. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat
  2. Add flour and mix until brown
  3. Add milk then wait for it to boil and turn it to simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Add about 1/2 of the gruyère cheese and stir until melted
  5. Season your cheese sauce with salt, pepper, and nutmeg

Directions for Sandwich

  1. Spread butter on the sliced bread
  2. Top each piece of bread with the cheese sauce, a slice of ham, dijon mustard, and the remaining gruyère cheese.
  3. Broil or toast your assembled bread until the cheese is melted and slightly browned
  4. Once they are toasted put the halves together to make a sandwich
  5. Cook an over-easy egg for each sandwich
  6. Season your eggs with whatever you want and place them on top of your sandwiches
