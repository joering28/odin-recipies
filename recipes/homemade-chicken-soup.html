Homemade Chicken Soup

What is Homemade Chicken Soup?

Homemade Chicken Soup is a simple soup that is great for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs. Chicken
  • 4 Carrots
  • 4 Celery Stalks
  • 1 Onion
  • Water
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Chicken Bouillon Cube

Directions

  1. Chop the carrots, celery, and onion in half
  2. Add all of the chicken and vegtables to a large pot
  3. Add enough water to cover the ingredients
  4. Bring to a boil then turn it to simmer for 90 minutes
  5. Strain soup while reserving the stock
  6. Chop the chicken and vegtables into the desired size
  7. Rinse the pot and add the ingredients and stock back in
  8. Put back on simmer and season to tastes with salt, pepper and chicken bouillon
