Tater Tot Casserole

What is Tater Tot Casserole?

Tater Tot Casserole is an easy dish that can be served as a main dish or a side.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 Can Cream of Mushroom
  • 1 Pack of Frozen Tater Tots
  • 2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350F degrees
  2. Cook ground beef in skillet and season with salt and pepper
  3. Add cream of mushroom to beef and stir
  4. Transfer beef to a 9x13 baking dish
  5. Layer tater tots evenly across the top
  6. Sprinkle cheddar cheese evenly across the top
  7. Bake in the oven for about 30 to 45 minutes until golden brown
